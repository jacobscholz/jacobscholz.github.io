Degree Pathways


OSU Cascades


Attractive Qualities

Offers a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science, with applied options in software engineering and software entrepeneurship.

Also offers a Minor in Computer Science.

The computer science degree programs are very straightforward, and OSU is a very reputable college. And since OSU Cascades is a smaller campus than OSU Corvallis, you'd probably learn better thanks to the smaller class sizes.

Degree Requirements

-Baccalaureate Core: 32-38 credits

-Computer Science: 97 credits

-Applied Computer Science Option: 32 credits

-Electives as needed to reach 180 credits, at least 60 upper division credits

Oregon Institute of Technology


Attractive Qualities

Offers Bachelor's Degrees in Applied Mathematics, Computer Engineering Technology, Cybersecurity, Data Science, Health Informatics, Embedded Systems Engineering Technology, Information Technology, and Software Engineering Technology.

There are tons of computer science degree options to choose from, and OIT has a great record of employment for its graduates.

Applied Mathematics Degree Requirements

-Lower Division: 18 credits

-Upper Division Core: 44 credits

-Upper Division Math/Physics Electives: 7 credits

-Two of the following: MATH 422, MATH 423, MATH 452, MATH 453

-Focused Electives: 16 credits
